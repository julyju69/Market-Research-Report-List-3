<p><p>Self-injection systems are divided into two main types: Fillable and Pre-Filled systems. Fillable systems require the user to manually fill the syringe with medication before each use, while pre-filled systems come pre-loaded with a single dose of medication. Fillable systems offer more flexibility in terms of dosage adjustments, but pre-filled systems are more convenient and reduce the risk of dosing errors. Both types of systems cater to different patient needs and preferences in the self-injection market.</p></p>
