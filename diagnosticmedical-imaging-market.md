<p><p>The diagnostic/medical imaging market is segmented into various types of imaging systems, including X-ray, CT scanners, ultrasound, MRI, and nuclear imaging systems. X-ray imaging systems use electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the inside of the body. CT scanners combine X-rays and computer technology to create detailed cross-sectional images. Ultrasound systems use sound waves to generate real-time images of internal organs. MRI systems use magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images. Nuclear imaging systems involve the use of radioactive substances to create images of the body's organs and tissues.</p></p>
