<p><p>Steel wire tire cord market types are categorized based on their tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) cords have standard strength, suitable for everyday use. High Tensile (HT) cords offer increased strength for enhanced performance. Super Tensile (ST) cords provide even greater strength, ideal for heavy duty applications. Ultra Tensile (UT) cords are the strongest, offering maximum durability and reliability, commonly used in specialty tires for extreme conditions. Each type caters to different needs in the tire industry, ensuring optimal performance and safety.</p></p>
