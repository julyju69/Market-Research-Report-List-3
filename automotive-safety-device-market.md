<p><p>Active safety systems in the automotive safety device market refer to technologies that help prevent accidents from occurring in the first place. This includes features like adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and automatic emergency braking. On the other hand, passive safety systems focus on minimizing the impact of a crash once it has occurred. This includes technologies like airbags, seat belts, and crumple zones. Both types of safety systems work together to ensure the safety of drivers and passengers on the road.</p></p>
