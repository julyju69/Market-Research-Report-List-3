Hydrosol, on the other hand, is the byproduct of the steam distillation process and is known for its gentler scent and skin-soothing properties. Both the oil and hydrosol markets cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the realm of natural and holistic healthcare and beauty products.</p></p>
