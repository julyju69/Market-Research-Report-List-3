<p><p>Transcatheter Mitral Valve Replacement (TMVR) is a minimally invasive procedure used to treat mitral valve regurgitation. There are different types of TMVR devices available in the market, including MitraClip, Edwards PASCAL, and Edwards SAPIEN 3. MitraClip is a clip-based system that grasps and repairs the mitral valve leaflets. Edwards PASCAL and Edwards SAPIEN 3 are both balloon-expandable valves that are implanted in the mitral valve to improve its function. These devices offer less invasive treatment options for patients with mitral valve disease.</p></p>
