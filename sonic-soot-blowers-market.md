There are two main types - Rotary Flute Type Sonic Soot Blowers and Diaphragm Type Sonic Soot Blowers. </p><p>Rotary Flute Type Sonic Soot Blowers use a rotating nozzle to create powerful acoustic waves to remove soot and ash buildup from boiler tubes. </p><p>Diaphragm Type Sonic Soot Blowers use a diaphragm driven by compressed air to produce high-frequency sound waves for cleaning purposes. Both types are effective in improving boiler efficiency and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
