<p><p>Zirconia dental products are widely used in the dental industry and can be classified into two main types: Zirconia Dental Disc and Zirconia Dental Block. Zirconia Dental Disc is a flat circular piece used for milling various dental restorations such as crowns and bridges. On the other hand, Zirconia Dental Block is a solid block of zirconia that is used in CAD/CAM systems for milling dental restorations. Both products offer high strength, aesthetic value, and biocompatibility, making them popular choices in modern dentistry.</p></p>
