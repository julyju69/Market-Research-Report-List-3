<p><p>Vacuum breakers are devices used to prevent backflow and siphoning of liquids in plumbing systems. There are two main types of vacuum breakers in the market: low pressure type and high pressure type. Low pressure vacuum breakers are designed for systems with a maximum pressure of 125 psi, while high pressure vacuum breakers are suitable for systems with pressures exceeding 125 psi. Both types work by allowing air to enter the system to break the vacuum and prevent contamination of the water supply.</p></p>
