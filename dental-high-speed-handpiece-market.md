<p><p>Dental High-Speed Handpiece market is divided into two main types: Inside Exhaust Handpiece and Outside Exhaust Handpiece. Inside exhaust handpieces have a built-in exhaust system that collects debris and water spray internally, preventing contamination and reducing maintenance needs. In contrast, Outside Exhaust Handpieces expel debris and water spray externally, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination and providing easier access for cleaning. Dentists can choose between these two types based on their preference for maintenance and infection control protocols.</p></p>
