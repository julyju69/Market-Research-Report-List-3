<p><p>Organic Almond Oil is available in two main types: Sweet Almond Oil and Bitter Almond Oil. Sweet Almond Oil is the most common type used for aromatherapy, massage, and skincare due to its mild and pleasant aroma. It is rich in vitamins and beneficial fatty acids. Bitter Almond Oil, on the other hand, contains a compound called amygdalin, which converts to cyanide in the body and is not suitable for internal use. It is primarily used in small quantities for its fragrance in perfumes and soaps.</p></p>
