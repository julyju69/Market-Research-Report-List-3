<p><p>Pump diode lasers have different types in the market - pulsed pump lasers, CW-pumped lasers, and others. Pulsed pump lasers provide high peak power output in short bursts, suitable for applications such as material processing. CW-pumped lasers emit continuous output for long periods, making them ideal for applications like surgery and telecommunications. Other types in the market include multi-mode pump lasers, single-mode pump lasers, and specialty pump lasers catering to specific industry needs. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
