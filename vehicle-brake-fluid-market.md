<p><strong>The Vehicle Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle Brake Fluid Market comprises various types such as DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is suitable for most vehicles and provides good performance under normal driving conditions. DOT 4 has a higher boiling point than DOT 3, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. DOT 5 is silicone-based and is typically used in high-performance vehicles. DOT 5.1 is a glycol-based fluid with a higher boiling point than DOT 4, offering improved performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
