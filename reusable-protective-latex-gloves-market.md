<p><p>Reusable protective latex gloves come in two main types: powdered gloves and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with cornstarch or other powders to make them easier to put on and take off, while non-powdered gloves do not have this coating. While powdered gloves are more common, non-powdered gloves are preferred in certain industries such as food processing and electronics, where powder residue can cause contamination. Both types offer protection against chemicals, pathogens, and other hazards, making them essential for various work environments.</p></p>
