<p><strong>The IoT Smart Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Diffused Silicon Type</li><li>Sapphire Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IoT Smart Pressure Sensors market includes three main types: Piezoelectric Type, Diffused Silicon Type, and Sapphire Type. Piezoelectric Type sensors use a crystal that generates an electric charge when pressure is applied. Diffused Silicon Type sensors utilize a silicon diaphragm that deforms under pressure, causing a change in resistance. Sapphire Type sensors are made from sapphire material and offer high precision and durability. Each type caters to different applications and industries, providing a variety of options for measuring pressure in IoT devices.</p></p>
