<p><p>Anesthesia Laryngeal Mask Airway (LMA) is a medical device used during surgeries as an alternative to endotracheal intubation. The demand for LMA is increasing due to its advantages such as ease of insertion, lower risk of airway trauma, and reduced postoperative discomfort.</p><p>The Anesthesia Laryngeal Mask Airway Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period.
