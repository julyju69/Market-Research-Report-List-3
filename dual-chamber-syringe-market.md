<p><p>The dual chamber syringe market can be divided into two main types: conventional and safety syringes. Conventional dual chamber syringes have two separate chambers for storing and mixing two different medications before administration. Safety dual chamber syringes, on the other hand, are designed with additional safety features to prevent needlestick injuries and accidental needle exposures. These safety syringes often include mechanisms such as needle shields or retractable needles to reduce the risk of healthcare workers being exposed to bloodborne pathogens.</p></p>
