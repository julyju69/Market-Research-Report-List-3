<p><p>Towline floor conveyors are a popular material handling solution used in various industries. On-floor towline conveyors are installed on the surface of the floor, allowing for easy installation and flexibility in layout changes. In-floor towline conveyors are embedded in the floor, providing a smooth surface for material movement and reducing obstructions in the work area.
