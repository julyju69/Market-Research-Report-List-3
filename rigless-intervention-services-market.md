<p><p>Rigless intervention services refer to operations performed on a well without the need for a drilling rig. The market is segmented into three main types: Coiled Tubing Service, which uses a continuous metal pipe to perform various operations in the wellbore; Wireline Service, which uses a single strand of wire to lower tools and equipment into the well; and Hydraulic Workover Service, which involves the use of hydraulic equipment to perform interventions in the wellbore. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different scenarios based on the requirements of the well.</p></p>
