Radioimmunoassay measures radioactive substances in a sample, and enzyme-linked immunoassay detects enzymes linked to antibodies. The consumables market includes reagents, kits, and accessories necessary for immunoassay testing. These instruments and analyzers play a crucial role in diagnosing various diseases and monitoring treatment effectiveness.</p></p>
