<p><p>Fixed POS terminals are stationary devices that are typically found at checkout counters in retail stores, restaurants, and other establishments. These terminals are permanently installed and connected to a central system for processing transactions. Portable POS terminals, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be carried around by sales associates to process payments on the go. They are used in industries such as events, food trucks, and pop-up shops where mobility is essential. Both types of terminals utilize biometric technology for secure and convenient payment authentication.</p></p>
