<p><p>Non-phthalate plasticizers are categorized into four main types - Adipates, Trimellitates, Epoxies, and Benzoates. Adipates are known for their excellent low-temperature properties and durability, making them suitable for applications in flooring and automotive interiors. Trimellitates offer exceptional heat stability and are commonly used in wire and cable insulation. Epoxies provide superior resistance to high temperatures and chemicals, making them ideal for coatings and sealants. Benzoates are preferred for their non-toxic properties and are often used in food packaging and medical devices.</p></p>
