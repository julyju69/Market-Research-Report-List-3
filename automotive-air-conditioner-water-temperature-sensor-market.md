Thermocouples type sensors rely on the principle of thermal electromotive force, RTDs type sensors use the change in electrical resistance with temperature, and Thermistors type sensors use the change in electrical resistance with temperature. Each type has its own advantages and applications in automotive air conditioning systems based on factors like accuracy, sensitivity, and cost.</p></p>
