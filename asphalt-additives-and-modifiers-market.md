<p><ul><li>Asphalt Additives</li><li>Asphalt Modifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Asphalt additives are chemical compounds added to asphalt mixtures to improve their properties and performance. These additives may include polymers, fibers, warm mix additives, anti-stripping agents, and rejuvenators. On the other hand, asphalt modifiers are substances that alter the physical properties of asphalt, such as viscosity, elasticity, and durability. These modifiers include polymers, crumb rubber, sulphur, and wax. Both asphalt additives and modifiers play a crucial role in enhancing the quality and longevity of asphalt pavements in various construction projects.</p></p>
