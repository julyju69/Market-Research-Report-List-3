<p><ul><li>Small (Upto 500 gpm)</li><li>Medium (500-1000 gpm)</li><li>High (More Than 1000 gpm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mining pumps market can be classified into three main types based on their flow rate capacity. Small pumps have a capacity of up to 500 gallons per minute (gpm), medium pumps range between 500-1000 gpm, and high pumps have a capacity of more than 1000 gpm. Each type caters to different mining operations based on their size and requirement for water handling. Small pumps are suitable for smaller mining sites, while high pumps are used in large-scale mining operations with higher water flow needs.</p></p>
