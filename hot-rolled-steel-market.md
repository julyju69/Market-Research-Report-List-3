<p><p>Hot rolled steel market consists of two main types - steel plate and steel coil markets. Steel plates are flat pieces of steel that have been heated and then pressed into a particular shape or thickness. They are used in various construction projects and industrial applications. Steel coils, on the other hand, are wound up rolls of steel that are produced through hot rolling process. They are commonly used in manufacturing industries for making machinery, automotive parts, and other products.</p></p>
