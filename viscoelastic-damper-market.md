<p><p>Viscoelastic damper market consists of various types of dampers used for vibration control and energy dissipation in structures. Flat dampers are placed between structures to absorb and dissipate energy, while cylindrical viscoelastic dampers provide damping in both axial and shear directions. Viscoelastic damping walls are large-scale structures filled with viscoelastic materials to provide damping and reduce vibrations in buildings and bridges. Each type of damper offers unique advantages for different applications in the construction and infrastructure industry.</p></p>
