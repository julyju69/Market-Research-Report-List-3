<p><p>Electromagnetic NDT Equipment refers to various types of inspection devices that use electromagnetic principles for detecting flaws or abnormalities in materials. Eddy-Current NDT Equipment employs electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface defects in conductive materials. Remote Field Testing Equipment is used for detecting flaws in ferrous and non-ferrous materials. Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing Equipment detects defects by measuring variations in magnetic flux. Alternating Current Field Measurement Equipment measures conductivity and permeability of materials. Each type of equipment has specific applications and advantages in the field of non-destructive testing.</p></p>
