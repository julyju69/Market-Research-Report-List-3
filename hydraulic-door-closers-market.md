<p><p>Hydraulic door closers are available in three main types: surface applied door closers, concealed door closers, and floor springs. Surface applied door closers are visible on the surface of the door and are commonly used in commercial buildings. Concealed door closers are hidden within the door frame for a more seamless appearance. Floor springs are installed in the floor beneath the door and offer a sleek and modern look. Each type of hydraulic door closer offers different benefits and is suited for various applications.</p></p>
