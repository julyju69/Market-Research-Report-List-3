<p><p>Air audit equipment market has several types including pressure meter, differential pressure meter, flow meter, leak detector, and others. Pressure meters are used to measure the pressure of air flow while differential pressure meters compare pressures between two different points. Flow meters are used to measure the flow rate of air, while leak detectors are utilized to identify and locate air leaks. Other types of air audit equipment may include particle counters, thermometers, anemometers, and air velocity meters.</p></p>
