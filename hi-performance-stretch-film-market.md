<p><p>Hi-Performance Stretch Film is available in two main market types: Manual Stretch Film and Machine Stretch Film. Manual Stretch Film is designed for hand wrapping applications, offering flexibility and ease of use. It is commonly used for smaller packaging operations. On the other hand, Machine Stretch Film is specifically formulated for automated wrapping machines, providing higher efficiency and consistency in wrapping pallets. Both types of stretch film offer high-performance qualities such as stretchability, durability, and puncture resistance to ensure secure packaging and protection of goods during transit.</p></p>
