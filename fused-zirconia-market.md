<p><p>The Fused Zirconia market includes two primary types of production methods: Electrically Fused To Form Zirconia and Chemical Synthesis of Zirconia. Electrically Fused To Form Zirconia involves the melting of zirconium oxide powders in an electric arc furnace to produce zirconia. On the other hand, Chemical Synthesis of Zirconia involves the chemical reaction of zirconium compounds to form zirconia. Both methods have their own advantages and are utilized in various industries for applications requiring high-performance ceramic materials.</p></p>
