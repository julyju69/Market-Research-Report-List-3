<p><p>Advanced Surgical Imaging System market includes Image intensifier C-arms and Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms). Image intensifier C-arms use traditional image intensifier technology to produce real-time X-ray images, while FPD C-arms use flat panel detectors for higher image quality and faster image acquisition. FPD C-arms are becoming more popular due to their superior image quality, reduced radiation dose, and flexibility in imaging options. Both types of systems are widely used in various surgical procedures for guidance and visualization.</p></p>
