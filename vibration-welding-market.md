<p><p>Vibration welding market types include Travel Welding Mode, Time Welding Mode, and other markets. In Travel Welding Mode, one of the parts remains stationary while the other moves back and forth, generating friction and heat for welding. Time Welding Mode involves both parts vibrating at the same frequency and amplitude for a set amount of time to achieve welding. Other markets in vibration welding may involve unique applications or variations in the welding process based on specific requirements or industries.</p></p>
