<p><p>Film permeability testers are used to determine the permeability of films to various gases, including water vapor, oxygen, and other gases. Water vapor permeability testers measure the rate at which water vapor can pass through a film, while oxygen permeability testers determine the oxygen transmission rate of a film. Other gas permeability testers assess the permeability of films to gases other than water vapor and oxygen. These tests are crucial for industries such as food packaging and pharmaceuticals to ensure product quality and shelf life.</p></p>
