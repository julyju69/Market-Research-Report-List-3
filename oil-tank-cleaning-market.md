<p><p>Oil tank cleaning can be done manually or automatically. Manual cleaning involves workers physically entering the tank to remove sludge and residue using tools and equipment. This method is labor-intensive and time-consuming but allows for thorough cleaning. On the other hand, automatic cleaning utilizes specialized machines and equipment that can clean the tank without the need for human entry. This method is faster and more efficient, reducing the risk of accidents and ensuring a more consistent level of cleanliness.</p></p>
