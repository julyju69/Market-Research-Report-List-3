<p><p>Right Angle Rolling Motor Market Types include Torque Controlled and Stall Torque markets. In Torque Controlled motors, the speed of the motor is controlled by the applied torque, allowing for precise control over the motor's movement. In Stall Torque motors, the motor's torque output is highest when the motor is prevented from rotating, making them ideal for applications requiring high torque at low speeds. Both types cater to different requirements and are widely used in various industries for specific applications.</p></p>
