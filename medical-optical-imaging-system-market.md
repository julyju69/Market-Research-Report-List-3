<p><p>The Medical Optical Imaging System market includes various types of imaging technologies such as Photoacoustic Tomography, Optical Coherence Tomography, Hyperspectral Imaging, and Near-infrared Spectroscopy. Photoacoustic Tomography utilizes laser-induced ultrasound to generate high-resolution images of tissues. Optical Coherence Tomography uses light waves to create cross-sectional images of tissue. Hyperspectral Imaging captures a spectrum of light from each pixel in an image for detailed analysis. Near-infrared Spectroscopy measures the absorption of near-infrared light to analyze tissue composition and oxygen levels.</p></p>
