Biopolymer implants are biocompatible and biodegradable. Hydrogels are water-based implants that mimic the properties of natural tissues. Hydroxyapatite implants strengthen bones and promote bone growth. Other types of bio-implants include synthetic materials or combinations of different materials for specific applications in non-surgical procedures.</p></p>
