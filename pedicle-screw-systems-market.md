<p><p>Pedicle screw systems are a type of spinal implant used in spinal fusion surgery to provide stability and promote fusion of vertebral bones. The three main types of pedicle screws are conical, cylindrical, and dual cored. Conical pedicle screws have a tapered design for easier insertion, while cylindrical screws have a straight design. Dual cored pedicle screws have two cores for improved stability and strength. Each type of pedicle screw offers unique features and benefits for surgeons to choose from based on patient needs and surgical preferences.</p></p>
