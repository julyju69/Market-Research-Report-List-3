<p><ul><li>Internal Fixators</li><li>External Fixators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trauma products market includes internal fixators and external fixators. Internal fixators are implants used inside the body to stabilize fractured bones, while external fixators are devices placed outside the body to provide stability and alignment to the affected area. Internal fixators are usually preferred for complex fractures as they offer better stability and reduce the risk of infection. External fixators, on the other hand, are used for temporary stabilization or in cases where internal fixation is not possible.</p></p>
