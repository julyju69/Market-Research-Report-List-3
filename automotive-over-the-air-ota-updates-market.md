<p><ul><li>SOTA</li><li>FOTA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Over the Air (OTA) Updates can be categorized into two main types: Software Over the Air (SOTA) and Firmware Over the Air (FOTA) updates. SOTA updates are concerned with updating the software systems of vehicles, such as infotainment systems or advanced driver assistance systems. On the other hand, FOTA updates focus on updating the firmware of vehicles, which includes the embedded software that controls the hardware components like engine management systems. Both types of updates allow for remote and convenient updating of vehicle systems without requiring physical intervention.</p></p>
