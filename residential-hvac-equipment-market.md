<p><p>Residential HVAC Equipment market comprises two main types: Direct Expansion Systems and Central Air Conditioning Systems. Direct Expansion Systems use refrigerant to cool the air directly, making them more efficient for smaller spaces. Central Air Conditioning Systems, on the other hand, use ductwork to distribute cool air throughout the entire home, making them ideal for larger homes. Both types offer efficient cooling solutions for residential spaces, with Direct Expansion Systems better suited for smaller areas and Central Air Conditioning Systems for larger spaces.</p></p>
