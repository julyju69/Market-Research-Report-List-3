Low Power lasers typically have an output of up to 100W and are commonly used in applications such as telecommunications and sensing. Mid-Power lasers have outputs ranging from 100W to 500W, and are commonly used in materials processing and medical applications. High Power lasers have outputs greater than 500W and are used in industrial cutting and welding applications where high precision and power are required.</p></p>
