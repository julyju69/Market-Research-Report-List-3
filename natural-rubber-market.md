<p><strong>The Natural Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ribbed Smoked Sheet (RSS)</li><li>Technically Specified Rubber (TSR)</li><li>Latex</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural rubber is classified into different market types based on processing methods and final product characteristics. Ribbed Smoked Sheet (RSS) is made by coagulating latex into sheets, drying them, and then smoking them. Technically Specified Rubber (TSR) is made by treating field latex with chemicals to improve its properties. Latex is a liquid form of natural rubber used in products like gloves and adhesives. Other market types include compound rubber products like reclaimed rubber and crepe rubber. Each type serves specific industries and applications within the natural rubber market.</p></p>
