<p><p>Accident recorders, also known as dash cams, come in two main market types: Single Channel and Dual Channel. Single Channel recorders capture footage from the front of the vehicle, while Dual Channel recorders have an additional camera that captures footage from the rear. Single Channel recorders are typically more affordable and simpler to install, while Dual Channel recorders offer more comprehensive coverage and additional features. Both types of recorders are valuable tools for providing evidence in the event of an accident.</p></p>
