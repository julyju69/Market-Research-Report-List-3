<p><p>Tabletop Electron Microscopes are advanced imaging tools used in various industries. The W Electron Microscope uses a tungsten filament as a source of electrons for imaging. The FEG Electron Microscope utilizes a field emission gun for sharper imaging and higher resolution. The FIB Electron Microscope combines imaging with focused ion beam for precision milling and imaging at the nanoscale level.
