<p><p>Ultrafine microsilica, also known as silica fume, is classified into three main types based on its density: Densified silica fume, semi-densified silica fume, and undensified silica fume. </p><p>Densified silica fume has been processed to achieve a high density, making it easier to handle and transport. Semi-densified silica fume is partially processed, offering a balance between density and ease of use. Undensified silica fume is in its raw form and requires additional processing before application. These different types cater to various preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
