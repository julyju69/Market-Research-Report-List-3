<p><p>Horizontal vibrating screens are classified into three main types based on the number of layers: Single-layer, Double-layer, and Three-layer horizontal vibrating screens. Single-layer screens are the simplest design, providing basic screening and classification functions. Double-layer screens offer increased efficiency by allowing for two separate screening processes simultaneously. Three-layer screens offer the highest level of screening precision and efficiency by incorporating an additional layer for finer particle separation. These different types cater to various industry needs and requirements.</p></p>
