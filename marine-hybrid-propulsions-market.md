<p><strong>The Marine Hybrid Propulsions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diesel-electric</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Serial Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine hybrid propulsions market consists of three main types: diesel-electric, parallel hybrid, and serial hybrid. Diesel-electric propulsion uses a combination of diesel engines and electric generators to power the ship's electric motors. Parallel hybrid systems combine traditional diesel engines with electric propulsion systems to improve efficiency. Serial hybrid systems feature a generator that charges a battery bank, which then powers the electric motor. Each type offers different advantages in terms of efficiency, emissions reduction, and overall performance in marine applications.</p></p>
