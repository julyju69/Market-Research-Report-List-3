<p><p>C-Reactive Protein (CRP) Test Kits are available in various types, including immunoturbidimetric, ELISA, CLIA, and other markets. Immunoturbidimetric CRP test kits measure the level of CRP by detecting the formation of antigen-antibody complexes. ELISA test kits use enzyme-linked antibodies to identify CRP levels. CLIA test kits utilize chemiluminescence to measure CRP in a sample. Other market types may include rapid tests or point-of-care devices for quick and convenient CRP testing.</p></p>
