<p><p>Agricultural product baler market includes two main types: round balers and square balers. Round balers generally produce cylindrical bales, which are more common for hay and silage. They offer easier handling and storage. Square balers, on the other hand, create square-shaped bales, which are usually preferred for crops like straw and alfalfa. They are known for producing denser bales, making them easier to stack and transport. Both types of balers cater to different needs and preferences of farmers in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
