<p><strong>The Air Conveyors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure</li><li>Negative Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air conveyors are classified into two main types based on the pressure they operate at: positive pressure and negative pressure. Positive pressure air conveyors use blowers to force air through the system, pushing materials along the conveyor line. On the other hand, negative pressure air conveyors use suction from fans to pull materials through the system. Positive pressure conveyors are commonly used for moving lightweight materials or capsules, while negative pressure conveyors are suitable for heavier materials or powders.</p></p>
