<p><strong>The Self Loading Concrete Mixers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Movable</li><li>Fixed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self loading concrete mixers can be classified into two main types: movable and fixed. </p><p>Movable self loading concrete mixers are designed to be easily transported from one job site to another, making them ideal for construction projects that require frequent relocation. These mixers typically have wheels or can be mounted on a truck for easy transportation.</p><p>On the other hand, fixed self loading concrete mixers are stationary units that are permanently installed at a construction site. They are commonly used for larger projects where a continuous and consistent supply of concrete is required. Fixed mixers are typically larger in size and have a higher capacity than movable mixers.</p></p>
