<p><strong>The Automobile Hydraulic Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3 Brake Fluids</li><li>DOT 4 Brake Fluids</li><li>DOT 5 Brake Fluids</li><li>DOT 5.1 Brake Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile hydraulic brake fluids are categorized into different types based on their composition and specifications. DOT 3 brake fluids are commonly used in most vehicles and offer moderate performance. DOT 4 brake fluids have a higher boiling point, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles. DOT 5 brake fluids are silicone-based and are not compatible with other types. DOT 5.1 brake fluids are suitable for modern vehicles with advanced brake systems and offer superior performance and stability under extreme conditions.</p></p>
