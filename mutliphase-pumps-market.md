<p><p>Multiphase pumps are specially designed to handle mixtures of gas, liquid, and solids in various industries such as oil & gas, chemical, and food processing. The three main types of multiphase pumps are Twin-screw pump (TSP), Helicoaxial pump (HAP), and Progressing-cavity pump (PCP). TSPs are known for their high efficiency and gentle pumping action, while HAPs are used in high-pressure applications. PCPs are versatile and can handle high-viscosity fluids with solids, making them suitable for challenging pumping environments.</p></p>
