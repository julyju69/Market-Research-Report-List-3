<p><p>IR windows are protective coverings installed on infrared imaging devices to allow infrared energy to pass through while keeping out contaminants. There are two main types of IR windows - Crystal Material and Polymer Material. Crystal Material windows are made from materials like germanium or sapphire, offering higher clarity and durability. Polymer Material windows are made from plastic materials, providing cost-effective options with decent infrared transmission properties. Both types of IR windows play a crucial role in ensuring the longevity and effectiveness of infrared imaging devices.</p></p>
