<p><p>Orthobiological products market consists of several types including Demineralized Bone Matrix (DBM), Allograft, Bone Morphogenetic Proteins (BMP), Viscosupplementation, Machined Bones, and other markets. DBM is processed human cadaver bones used for bone regeneration. Allografts are bone tissue from a donor used for bone grafting. BMP are proteins that stimulate bone growth. Viscosupplementation involves injecting lubricating fluid into joints to reduce pain. Machined bones are custom-made implants. Other markets include stem cell therapy, platelet-rich plasma, and synthetic bone grafts.</p></p>
