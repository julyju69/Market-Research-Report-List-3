<p><p>Enteral feeding sets market is segmented into different types based on the method of administration. Gastrostomy feeding involves delivering nutrition directly into the stomach through a surgically created opening. Jejunostomy feeding delivers nutrition directly into the small intestine. Nasoenteric feeding involves delivering nutrition through a feeding tube inserted through the nose and into the gastrointestinal tract. Other types of enteral feeding sets include transgastric feeding and percutaneous endoscopic gastrostomy, among others. Each type caters to different patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
