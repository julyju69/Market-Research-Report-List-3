<p><p>Textured coatings are commonly categorized into four main types: smooth textured coating, sand textured coating, coarse textured coating, and others. Smooth textured coating provides a sleek and uniform finish, while sand textured coating offers a grittier surface similar to sandpaper. Coarse textured coating is more rugged and has a rougher feel compared to the other types. The "others" category typically includes unique or specialty textured coatings that do not fall into the previous three classifications.</p></p>
