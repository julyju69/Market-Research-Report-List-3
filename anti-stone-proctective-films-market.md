<p><p>Anti-Stone Protective Films are available in different types such as Shrink Film and Stretch Film. Shrink Film is a type of protective film that shrinks when heat is applied, providing a tight and secure covering over the surface of the stone. Stretch Film, on the other hand, is a stretchable and flexible protective film that conforms to the shape of the stone, providing a protective barrier against scratches, dust, and other damage. Both types of films are commonly used in the construction and transportation industries to protect stone surfaces during handling and storage.</p></p>
