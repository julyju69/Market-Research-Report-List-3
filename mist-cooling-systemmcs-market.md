<p><p>Mist Cooling System (MCS) has three main market types based on pressure levels: Low Pressure MCS, Medium Pressure MCS, and High Pressure MCS. Low Pressure MCS operates at pressures below 250 psi, providing a gentle misting effect suitable for residential use. Medium Pressure MCS functions between 250-1000 psi, offering a balance of cooling efficiency and energy consumption. High Pressure MCS operates above 1000 psi, producing a fine mist that evaporates quickly for maximum cooling in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
