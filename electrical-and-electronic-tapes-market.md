<p><p>The electrical and electronic tapes market can be classified into various types based on the materials used. Cloth electrical tape is made of woven cotton fabric coated with a layer of adhesive for insulation purposes. PVC electrical tape is made of polyvinyl chloride and is primarily used for electrical insulation and wire bundling. PET electrical tape is made of polyester and offers excellent temperature resistance. Other types of tapes in the market include rubber electrical tape, silicone electrical tape, and self-fusing tape, each offering unique properties for different applications.</p></p>
