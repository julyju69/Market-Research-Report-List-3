<p><p>Multifunctional Optical Clear Adhesive (OCA) is available in two main types: Resistive OCA and Capacitive OCA. Resistive OCA is commonly used in resistive touch screens, providing high transparency and optical clarity while maintaining flexibility and durability. On the other hand, Capacitive OCA is specifically designed for capacitive touch screens, offering excellent touch sensitivity and conductivity. Both types of OCAs play a crucial role in various electronic devices, providing a secure and reliable bonding solution for displays and touchscreens.</p></p>
