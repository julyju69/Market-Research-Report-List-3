<p><p>Vacuum Gas Oil (VGO) refers to a type of feedstock used in refineries to produce high-value products like gasoline and diesel. VGO is categorized into two main market types: Heavy VGO (HVGO) and Light VGO (LVGO). HVGO is a heavier, more viscous form of VGO with a higher boiling point, which is used to produce products like diesel fuel. On the other hand, LVGO is a lighter, less viscous form of VGO with a lower boiling point, often used for producing gasoline.</p></p>
