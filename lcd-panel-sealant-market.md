<p><p>LCD Panel Sealant Market can be divided into two types based on viscosity: High Viscosity and Low Viscosity. High Viscosity sealants are thicker and more suitable for applications where a stronger bond and more resistance to movement is required. On the other hand, Low Viscosity sealants are more flowable and easier to apply, making them ideal for applications where a thinner layer of sealant is needed or where faster curing times are desired. Both types cater to different requirements in the LCD panel industry.</p></p>
