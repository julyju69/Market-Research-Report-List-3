<p><strong>The Spring Mattresses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonnell coils</li><li>Offset coils</li><li>Continuous coils</li><li>Marshall coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spring mattresses come in various market types based on the type of coils used in their construction. Bonnell coils are hourglass-shaped and offer good support. Offset coils have a hinged design for enhanced contouring and reduced motion transfer. Continuous coils consist of a single looped wire for consistent support across the mattress. Marshall coils are individually wrapped coils that conform to the body's shape for personalized comfort. Each market type offers unique benefits, catering to different preferences and needs.</p></p>
