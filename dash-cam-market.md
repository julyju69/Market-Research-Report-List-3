<p><p>Single Channel Dash Cameras are designed to record video footage from only one perspective. These cameras are typically mounted on the front windshield of a vehicle to capture what is happening ahead of the driver. On the other hand, Multi-Channel Dash Cameras have multiple lenses that can record video footage from various angles, providing a more comprehensive view of the surrounding environment. These cameras are often used by commercial drivers or fleet managers to monitor multiple areas of a vehicle at once.</p></p>
