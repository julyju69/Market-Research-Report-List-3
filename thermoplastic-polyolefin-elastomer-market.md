<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyolefin Elastomer, also known as TPO, is a popular material in the automotive and construction industries due to its flexibility and durability. Within the TPO market, there are two main types - Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM). EPM TPOs are known for their resistance to heat, chemicals, and weathering, making them ideal for roofing membranes and automotive components. EPDM TPOs, on the other hand, are highly flexible and offer excellent resistance to ozone and UV exposure, making them great for gaskets and seals.</p></p>
