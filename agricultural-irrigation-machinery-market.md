<p><p>Agricultural Irrigation Machinery market includes various types such as Sprinkler Irrigation and Drip Irrigation. Sprinkler Irrigation involves spraying water over crops in a circular motion, mimicking the natural rainfall pattern. It is suitable for larger areas and offers uniform water distribution. Drip Irrigation, on the other hand, delivers water directly to the roots of plants through a network of pipes and tubing. It is more water-efficient and precise, making it ideal for areas with water scarcity or plants that require controlled watering.</p></p>
