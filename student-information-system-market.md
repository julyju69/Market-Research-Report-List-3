<p><p>Student Information System (SIS) can be offered in two main market types: Cloud and On-Premise. Cloud-based SIS are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, providing flexibility and scalability. On-Premise SIS are installed and managed on the organization's own servers, offering more control over data security and customization. Cloud SIS is popular for its ease of use and cost-effectiveness, while On-Premise SIS is preferred by institutions with specific security and compliance requirements. Both types cater to different needs within the education sector.</p></p>
