Camera-Based Systems use cameras to provide a visual display of areas surrounding the vehicle, Radar-Based Systems use radar sensors to detect objects in the vehicle's blind spots, and Ultrasonic-Based Systems use ultrasonic sensors to measure distances between the vehicle and nearby objects.
