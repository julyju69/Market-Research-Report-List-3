<p><p>Coal cutters are tools used in the mining industry to extract coal from the earth efficiently. There are four main types of coal cutters in the market - Sawing Type, Planing Type, Drilling Type, and Impact Type. Sawing Type coal cutters use a rotating saw blade to cut through coal, while Planing Type cutters scrape the coal using a cutting drum. Drilling Type cutters bore holes into the coal, and Impact Type cutters break the coal through impact force. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific mining requirements.</p></p>
