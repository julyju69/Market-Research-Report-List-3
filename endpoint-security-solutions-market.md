<p><p>Endpoint Security Solutions can be categorized into two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-based. On-Premise solutions involve installing and managing security software on local servers and devices within an organization's network. This offers better control and customization but requires more maintenance. Cloud-based solutions, on the other hand, involve hosting security software on remote servers accessed through the internet. This provides scalability and flexibility, with updates and maintenance managed by the service provider. Both types offer protection for endpoints such as laptops, desktops, and mobile devices.</p></p>
