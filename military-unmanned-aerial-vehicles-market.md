<p><p>Military Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) are classified into two main types based on their configuration: Fixed Wing UAVs and Rotary Wing UAVs. Fixed Wing UAVs resemble traditional airplanes and are known for their long endurance, high speed, and large operational range. In contrast, Rotary Wing UAVs are designed with rotors similar to helicopters, providing vertical takeoff and landing capabilities and greater maneuverability in confined spaces. Both types play a crucial role in modern military operations by providing intelligence, surveillance, reconnaissance, and combat capabilities.</p></p>
