<p><p>The automotive suspension market consists of various types of components that are crucial for providing a smooth and stable driving experience. Coil springs are used to absorb shocks and maintain vehicle height. Leaf springs are commonly used in rear suspension systems to support heavy loads. Stabilizer bars help reduce body roll during cornering. Suspension arms connect the wheels to the chassis, allowing for movement and flexibility. Other components such as shock absorbers, struts, and control arms also play essential roles in maintaining vehicle stability and comfort.</p></p>
