<p><p>Skin filler market types include hyaluronic acid (HA), calcium hydroxylapatite (CaHA), poly-L-lactic acid (PLLA), polymethylmethacrylate (PMMA), and other fillers. HA fillers are popular for their natural compatibility with the skin and ability to add volume. CaHA fillers stimulate collagen production for long-lasting results. PLLA fillers are biodegradable and promote collagen growth over time. PMMA fillers are permanent and provide structural support. Other fillers may include collagen-based fillers or autologous fat injections. Each type offers unique benefits for rejuvenating and enhancing the skin.</p></p>
