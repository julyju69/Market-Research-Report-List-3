<p><strong>The Magnetic Compass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-reading</li><li>Distant-reading</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-reading magnetic compasses are handheld devices that provide immediate readings of magnetic direction. They are commonly used in navigation on smaller vessels and by hikers and campers. Distant-reading magnetic compasses, on the other hand, are installed on larger vessels and vehicles and provide readings from a distance, usually on a control panel or monitor. They are more complex and accurate than direct-reading compasses, making them suitable for use in high-tech navigation systems and advanced equipment.</p></p>
