<p><p>Zirconia ceramic blocks are categorized based on their translucency levels which determine their aesthetic and functional properties. Low translucency zirconia ceramic blocks are ideal for posterior restorations due to their high strength and durability. Medium translucency zirconia ceramic blocks provide a balance between strength and esthetics, making them suitable for both anterior and posterior restorations. High translucency zirconia ceramic blocks offer the best esthetics, mimicking natural teeth, and are mainly used for anterior restorations where a lifelike appearance is desired.</p></p>
