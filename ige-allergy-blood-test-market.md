<p><p>IgE allergy blood test market types include ELISA testing, Fluorescent Enzyme Immunoassays (FEIA) testing, and Chemiluminescent immunoassays testing. ELISA testing is commonly used due to its simplicity and cost-effectiveness in detecting IgE antibodies. FEIA testing uses fluorescent markers to enhance sensitivity and accuracy compared to ELISA. Chemiluminescent immunoassays testing utilizes light-emitting reactions for precise measurement of IgE levels. Each type offers specific advantages in diagnosing IgE allergies, catering to different needs and preferences in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
