<p><p>Plasma treatment systems can be classified into two main types based on temperature: high temperature and low temperature systems. High temperature plasma treatment systems utilize heat to efficiently treat surfaces, making them ideal for applications requiring intense and rapid treatment. In contrast, low temperature plasma treatment systems operate at cooler temperatures, making them suitable for delicate materials or heat-sensitive substrates. Both types offer unique advantages for various industrial applications, such as surface activation, cleaning, and coating processes.</p></p>
