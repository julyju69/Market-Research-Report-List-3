<p><p>Portable pyrometers are commonly categorized into two types: optical technology and infrared technology. Optical pyrometers measure temperature based on the intensity of visible light emitted by the object. They are commonly used in industrial processes where accurate temperature measurement is crucial. In contrast, infrared pyrometers measure temperature by detecting the infrared radiation emitted by the object. They are popular for their non-contact measurement capability and are commonly used in various industries such as metal processing, ceramics, and food processing.</p></p>
