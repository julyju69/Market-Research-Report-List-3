<p><p>Tray sealing machinery is available in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual tray sealing machines require human intervention for each step of the sealing process, making them suitable for smaller businesses with lower production volume. Semi-automatic machines are partially automated, requiring some manual input but with some automated functions. Fully automatic tray sealing machines are highly automated, requiring minimal human intervention and are suitable for high-volume production. Each type offers different levels of efficiency and automation to accommodate various production needs.</p></p>
