<p><ul><li>Scraper</li><li>Brush</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt cleaning systems, such as scrapers and brushes, are essential in maintaining efficient conveyor belts in various industries. Scraper systems use a metal blade to remove material stuck to the belt, while brush systems utilize rotating bristles to clean the surface. Both types help prevent residue buildup, improve belt longevity, and reduce maintenance costs. Scraper systems are ideal for heavy-duty applications, while brush systems are commonly used for delicate materials or in environments where water or chemicals are not suitable for cleaning.</p></p>
