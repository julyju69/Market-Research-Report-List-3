<p><ul><li>Bulk Forming</li><li>Sheet Forming</li><li>Powder Metal Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal forming equipment market is classified into three main types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming includes processes like forging, extrusion, and rolling, where metal is shaped by deforming it. Sheet forming involves processes like bending, stamping, and deep drawing to create complex shapes from metal sheets. Powder metal forming involves compacting metal powders in a die to create near-net shape parts. Each type of forming has its own unique set of equipment and processes to suit different metal forming applications.</p></p>
