<p><p>Security interlocking doors are available in two main types: automatic and non-return. Automatic interlocking doors are equipped with sensors and motors that automatically open and close the doors when triggered by a valid access control system. Non-return interlocking doors, on the other hand, require manual operation to open and close, making them ideal for areas where controlled access is needed. Both types of security interlocking doors provide a secure barrier against unauthorized entry while allowing for smooth and efficient flow of authorized personnel.</p></p>
