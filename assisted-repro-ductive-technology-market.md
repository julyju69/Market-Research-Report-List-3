ICSI involves injecting a single sperm into an egg to facilitate fertilization. PGD is a genetic test performed on embryos before implantation to identify genetic disorders. IUI involves placing sperm directly into a woman's uterus. ICI is similar but involves insemination into the cervix. Other technologies in the market include egg freezing, sperm donation, and surrogacy.</p></p>
