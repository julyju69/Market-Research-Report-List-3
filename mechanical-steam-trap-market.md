<p><p>Mechanical steam traps are devices used in steam systems to remove condensate and air without letting steam escape. There are a few different types on the market, including float type traps (lever float and free float) and inverted bucket traps. Float type traps work by using a float to open and close a valve, while inverted bucket traps use the weight of the bucket to open and close the valve. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on their unique mechanisms.</p></p>
