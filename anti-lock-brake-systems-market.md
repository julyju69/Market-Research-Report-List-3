<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Anti-lock Brake Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-channel ABS</li><li>Two-channel ABS</li><li>Three-channel ABS</li><li>Four-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-lock Brake Systems (ABS) are available in four main types based on the number of channels used for brake control: One-channel ABS, Two-channel ABS, Three-channel ABS, and Four-channel ABS. One-channel ABS provides control for one pair of wheels, typically found in motorcycles. Two-channel ABS controls the front and rear wheels separately, common in compact cars. Three-channel ABS controls one pair of wheels and the front wheels independently. Four-channel ABS offers individual control for each wheel, providing the highest level of stability and safety, commonly found in luxury vehicles.</p></p>
