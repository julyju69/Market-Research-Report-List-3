<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plenoptic Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Focused Plenoptic Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standard plenoptic cameras capture a single image with multiple focused depths, allowing for post-capture adjustments such as refocusing and depth mapping. They are commonly used in photography and computer vision applications. Focused plenoptic cameras, on the other hand, capture multiple images focused at different depths simultaneously, resulting in higher resolution and better image quality. They are often used in medical imaging and scientific research where precise depth information is crucial. Both types of plenoptic cameras offer unique capabilities for various industries and applications.</p></p>
