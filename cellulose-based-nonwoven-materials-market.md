<p><strong>The Cellulose-based Nonwoven Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunlace Methods</li><li>Needle-punched Methods</li><li>Meltblowing Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellulose-based nonwoven materials market includes spunlace methods, needle-punched methods, and meltblowing methods. Spunlace methods involve high-pressure water jets to entangle fibers. Needle-punched methods use barbed needles to interlock fibers. Meltblowing methods melt polymer pellets and extrude them into fine fibers. These methods cater to various industries such as hygiene, healthcare, and automotive due to their properties like absorbency, strength, and breathability. The market for cellulose-based nonwoven materials continues to grow due to their sustainable and biodegradable nature.</p></p>
