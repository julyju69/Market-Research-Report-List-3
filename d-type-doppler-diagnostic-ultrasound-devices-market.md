<p><strong>The D-type Doppler Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CW</li><li>PW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CW (Continuous Wave) Doppler ultrasound devices produce a continuous stream of sound waves, allowing for the measurement of high velocities. This type of Doppler ultrasound is often used in cardiology to assess blood flow through the heart and major blood vessels. </p><p>PW (Pulsed Wave) Doppler ultrasound devices emit short bursts of sound waves, enabling measurement of blood flow at specific locations. PW Doppler is commonly used in obstetrics, vascular studies, and general diagnostic imaging. Each type of Doppler ultrasound device has its own set of advantages and applications in the medical field.</p></p>
