<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Granular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor molding compounds are materials used to protect semiconductor devices and provide mechanical support. There are two main types of molding compounds on the market- liquid and granular. Liquid molding compounds are pre-mixed, flowable materials that are injected into molds to encapsulate the semiconductor device. Granular molding compounds are solid materials that are melted and then injected into molds. Both types offer advantages such as excellent thermal conductivity, high dielectric strength, and superior protection against moisture and contaminants.</p></p>
