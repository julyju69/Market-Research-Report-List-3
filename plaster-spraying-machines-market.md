<p><p>Plaster spraying machines are available in various types on the market. Pneumatic sprayers are powered by compressed air, providing a consistent and efficient spray. Worm-drive pump sprayers rely on a gear mechanism to transfer plaster from the hopper to the nozzle. Peristaltic pump sprayers use rotating rollers to push material through a hose. There are also other types of plaster spraying machines that operate on different mechanisms. Each type has its own unique benefits and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
