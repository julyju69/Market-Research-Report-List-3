Water Atomization Technology involves dispersing molten iron into fine droplets that solidify into powder when they come into contact with water. Gas Atomization Technology, on the other hand, uses high-pressure gas to atomize molten iron into fine powder particles. Each technology has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the market for iron powder products.</p></p>
