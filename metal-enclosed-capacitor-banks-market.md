<p><p>Metal enclosed capacitor banks are categorized based on their voltage levels into LV (Low Voltage), MV (Medium Voltage), and HV (High Voltage) capacitor banks. LV capacitor banks are typically used for residential and small commercial applications, MV capacitor banks are used for medium-sized industrial applications, and HV capacitor banks are used for large industrial and utility applications. Each type of capacitor bank is designed to operate efficiently at its specific voltage level, providing power factor correction and improving overall system efficiency.</p></p>
