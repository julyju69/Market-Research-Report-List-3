<p><p>Plastic healthcare and medical packaging market includes two main types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Rigid packaging provides better protection and durability for medical products but is typically more expensive. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and quality of healthcare and medical products.</p></p>
