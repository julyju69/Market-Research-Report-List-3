<p><p>In the car smart start key market, there are two main types: 1 way and 2 way systems. 1 way systems allow the key fob to send signals to the vehicle to start or stop the engine, but do not receive any feedback. On the other hand, 2 way systems can both send commands to the vehicle and receive feedback, such as confirming whether the engine has successfully started. This added functionality provides users with greater peace of mind and control over their vehicle.</p></p>
