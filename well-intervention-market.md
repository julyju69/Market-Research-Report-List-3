<p><strong>The Well Intervention Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light Intervention</li><li>Heavy Intervention</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Well intervention services can be categorized into two main types: light intervention and heavy intervention. Light intervention services typically involve simpler and less time-consuming operations such as wireline interventions, slickline interventions, and hydraulic workovers. On the other hand, heavy intervention services involve more complex and time-intensive operations such as coiled tubing interventions, snubbing operations, and hydraulic fracturing. Both types of interventions are essential for maintaining and optimizing the production of oil and gas wells.</p></p>
