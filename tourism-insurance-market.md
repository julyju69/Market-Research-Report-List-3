<p><p>Tourism insurance covers various aspects of travel-related risks. Medical Expense insurance covers medical costs incurred during the trip. Trip Cancellation insurance reimburses prepaid expenses if the trip is canceled. Trip Delay insurance provides coverage for expenses incurred due to delayed travel. Property Damage insurance covers damage or loss of personal belongings during the trip. Other types of tourism insurance may include coverage for emergency evacuation, trip interruption, and liability protection. These different types of insurance ensure travelers are protected from a range of unforeseen circumstances during their travels.</p></p>
