<p><p>Software-Defined Wide Area Network (SD-WAN) is a technology that enables the simplification and optimization of Wide Area Networks (WANs) by separating the network hardware from its control mechanism. SD-WAN offers benefits such as improved network visibility, increased security, and cost savings.</p><p>The SD-WAN market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period. <p><p>SD-WAN technology offers flexibility in deployment options, with two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based SD-WAN involves using a service provider's infrastructure to manage and optimize network traffic, providing scalability and reduced hardware costs. On-premise SD-WAN, on the other hand, requires deploying the technology within the organization's own infrastructure, offering more control over security and customization. Both market types cater to different business needs, with cloud-based solutions being ideal for scalability and cost-effectiveness, while on-premise solutions are preferred for enhanced security and customization options.</p></p>
