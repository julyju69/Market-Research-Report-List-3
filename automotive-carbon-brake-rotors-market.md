<p><p>The Automotive Carbon Brake Rotors Market consists of two main types - Carbon-Carbon Matrix and Carbon-Ceramic Matrix. Carbon-Carbon Matrix brake rotors are made of a composite material with carbon fibers embedded in a carbon matrix, providing high thermal conductivity and excellent performance under extreme heat conditions. On the other hand, Carbon-Ceramic Matrix brake rotors are made using a combination of carbon fibers and ceramic materials, offering improved durability and resistance to wear and corrosion, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
