<p><p>Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Systems market includes two major types of membranes: Polyaromatic Polymers Membrane and Partially Fluorinated Polymers Membrane. Polyaromatic Polymers Membrane are known for their high thermal and chemical stability, making them suitable for high-temperature operation. Partially Fluorinated Polymers Membrane, on the other hand, offer excellent proton conductivity and are more cost-effective compared to fully fluorinated membranes. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in PEM fuel cells, electrolyzers, and other applications requiring efficient proton exchange processes.</p></p>
