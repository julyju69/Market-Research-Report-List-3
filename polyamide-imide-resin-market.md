<p><strong>The Polyamide-imide Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acid Chloride Route</li><li>Diisocyanate Route</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyamide-imide resin market can be classified into two main types based on the production process: acid chloride route and diisocyanate route. In the acid chloride route, the resin is derived from the reaction between an acid chloride and an aromatic diamine, resulting in a high-performance material with excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance. On the other hand, in the diisocyanate route, the resin is produced by reacting a diisocyanate with an aromatic diamine, leading to a resin with similar high-performance properties suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
