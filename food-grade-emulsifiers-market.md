<p><p>Food Grade Emulsifiers are substances that help blend ingredients that normally don't mix together, such as oil and water. There are two main types of Food Grade Emulsifiers- Ionic Emulsifiers and Nonionic Emulsifiers. Ionic Emulsifiers have a charge and are attracted to water or oil molecules, while Nonionic Emulsifiers do not have a charge and are more versatile in blending different types of ingredients. Both types are commonly used in the food industry to improve texture, stability, and shelf life of various products.</p></p>
